1. stage4_tuned_colmap
2. stage5b_ft

- Fixed-pose COLMAP sparse initialization instead of stronger monocular-depth initialization.
- Cross-checkpoint staged training instead of merging stage4/stage5 into one optimization trajectory.
- Sparse-guided weak regularization in stage5b_ft, with multiview disabled.
